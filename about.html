About our great team

Welcome all you food bloggies! We’re Jason Heng, Jason Tan,and Muhammad Sohaib, the creators of Food Blogger Pro. Our Food Blog had been steadily growing over the last few years. I had been writing blog income reports. Jason Tan had written an eBook on food photography. Sohaib took photo and video about the food. We were getting more emails and more questions about food blogging from other food bloggers and people who wanted to start their own blog.
